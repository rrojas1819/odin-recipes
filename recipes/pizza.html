Pizza

Description

You just take the pizza from your fridge, and heat it up!

If not then you make the pizza yourself!!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. First take the dough and spread it apart!
  2. Now spread the tomato paste and cheese over it
  3. Now heat it up in a oven
  4. Enjoy!!
